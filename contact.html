Tube Amp Repair - Vintage or Modern

At FYD, we've been repairing and servicing all types of tube gear for over twenty-five years. We will even modify gear for tone and/or repurposing.

We work on amps by Fender, Marshall, Mesa Boogie, Crate, Peavey and Ampeg to name a few.

Our specialties include guitar and bass amps, preamps, outboard studio gear, audiophile equipment and even old radios. If you have something unusual and you need it fixed up, give us a call.
